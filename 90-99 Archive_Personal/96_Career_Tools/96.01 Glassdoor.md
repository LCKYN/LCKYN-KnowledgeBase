# Glassdoor

**Glassdoor** is a job search platform with company reviews, salary data, and interview insights from current and former employees.

ðŸ”— https://www.glassdoor.com/

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Company reviews | Anonymous employee ratings and feedback |
| Salary data | Compensation benchmarks by role and location |
| Interview questions | Real interview experiences and questions |
| Job listings | Search and apply to open positions |
| Company comparisons | Side-by-side company metrics |

## Use Cases

- **Salary negotiation** - Research market rates before offers
- **Interview prep** - Review company-specific interview questions
- **Company research** - Evaluate culture before applying
- **Career planning** - Compare roles and growth opportunities

## Key Metrics to Check

| Metric | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Overall rating | Quick culture health check |
| CEO approval | Leadership quality indicator |
| Recommend to friend | Employee satisfaction signal |
| Interview difficulty | Preparation calibration |
| Salary ranges | Negotiation baseline |
